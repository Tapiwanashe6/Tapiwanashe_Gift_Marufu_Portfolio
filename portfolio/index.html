A short introduction.

( 03 / Process )

How I work — four honest stages.

Step 01

Listen first

Stakeholder interviews, user conversations, and a hard look at the problem. No solutioning for the first ten days.

NotionDovetailMiro
Step 02

Sketch loudly

Low-fidelity flows on paper, then digital wireframes. I prefer ten bad ideas out loud to one perfect one kept inside.

PaperFigmaWhimsical
Step 03

Design carefully

Hi-fi mockups with a lightweight design system. Every decision is documented so the team can keep shipping without me.

FigmaTokensZeroheight
Step 04

Ship, then learn

Prototype, usability tests, ship, measure. Design is never finished — I stay close through the first two release cycles.
