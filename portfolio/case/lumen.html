( 01 ) Context

Most study apps assume broadband and a laptop. Most students have neither.

Lumen was commissioned by a Rwandan ed-tech non-profit reaching students in secondary schools across the Northern Province. Median device: a 2019 Tecno with 2GB of RAM. Median connection: intermittent 2G. Median screen time before battery anxiety: 40 minutes.

( 02 ) Constraints as design

< 8MB
APK size budget
2G
Worst-case sync target
100%
Core loops work offline

Every design choice was measured in kilobytes. Illustrations were replaced with simple line marks. The typeface was subset to just the characters used across five subjects. Animations were kept to opacity only — no transforms that force repaint on cheap GPUs.
