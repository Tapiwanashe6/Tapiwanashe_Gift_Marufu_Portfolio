( 01 ) Context

Most non-profits ask for trust. We wanted to offer proof instead.

Rwanda Cancer Relief supports patients and caregivers across three provinces. Their old donation page was a single-amount form with no context. Conversion was low, repeat giving lower still, and the team spent evenings writing thank-you emails by hand.

My six-week contract was framed simply: make giving feel like joining something, not clicking a button.

( 02 ) The "care circle"

We introduced the idea of a care circle — a small, named group of patients and caregivers in a specific district. Donors join a circle, not an abstract cause, and receive monthly updates from that specific group.
