( 01 ) Context

A freelancer in Kigali loses 6–9% on every USD payout. Meridian closes the gap.

East-African freelancers are increasingly paid in foreign currency by Western clients, but the last mile — converting to local currency and spending it — eats between 6% and 9% in combined wire, intermediary and FX fees. Existing local banks still treat freelancers like small businesses: paperwork-heavy, branch-first.

( 02 ) The brief

A three-page brief, essentially: "Open an account in 5 minutes. Get paid in any currency. Cash out to mobile money in under a day. Show me every fee before I commit."
